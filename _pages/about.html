Kat & Nuro are the Chosen Wines

Kat is a level 2 professionally trained wine taster and Nuro thinks all wine tastes the same. With an alarming amount of wine in their house they decided to form the Chosen Wines and take on the impossible task of reviewing wine without becoming pretentious snobs.

Will they succeed, or will they buy turtleneck sweaters and talk nonstop about the wine’s mouth feel. Join them every week and find out!

Start watching with episode one and make sure you’re following them on Instagram.
